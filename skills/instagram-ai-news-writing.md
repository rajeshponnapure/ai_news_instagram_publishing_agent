# Instagram AI News Writing Skill

Use this skill whenever the agent turns an email or article into an Instagram carousel caption or slide summary.

## Voice

- Write like a sharp human editor explaining AI news to creators, founders, developers, and curious tech people.
- Keep the language simple, vivid, and specific.
- Prefer active sentences: "OpenAI is making..." instead of "It was announced that..."
- Do not sound like a press release, SEO article, or generic AI summary.
- Do not invent facts. If the article does not say it, do not add it.

## Slide Summary Pattern

For each news item:

1. Start with the actual change: what launched, changed, broke, improved, or matters.
2. Explain who it affects: developers, creators, businesses, users, researchers, investors, etc.
3. Explain why it matters in plain English.
4. End with what to watch next: adoption, pricing, rollout, competition, limitations, or risk.

## Instagram Style Rules

- Strong first sentence, but no clickbait.
- Use concrete nouns and verbs.
- Avoid filler like "game-changing", "revolutionary", "in today's fast-paced world", and "dive into".
- Avoid repeating the headline in the body.
- Use short paragraphs for readability.
- The final caption must include a disclaimer that the post is AI-assisted and based on linked sources.

## Image Rules

- Prefer the article's `og:image` or `twitter:image`.
- If no social image exists, use the largest meaningful article image.
- Avoid logos, icons, tracking pixels, avatars, and decorative images.
- Fit the full image into the 1080x1350 layout without cropping important content.
